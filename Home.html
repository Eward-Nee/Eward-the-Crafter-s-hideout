Why a total redo?:

Well, I love to redo projects I made that is not perfect. It helps me to improve my skills if I do a project over and over again. I Just dont believe in dead projects. I also don't believe in a perfect program. There will always be something wrong, thus will there always be something to improve.

I believe that a programmer is as mush worth as his website, so I will always do my best to try and make my website as good as possible. You can expect many redos of my website, because im am still learning web-development. I will be adding new features as I learn them

I guess what I am trying to say is that you must please stay tuned as I learn new skills and put them to practise on my website.
